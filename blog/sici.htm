Introduction

The Sicilian Defense begins with the moves:

1. e4 c5

It is one of the most popular defenses against 1.e4, leading to dynamic and tactical play.

Popular Variations

  • Najdorf Variation: One of the sharpest lines.
  • Dragon Variation: Characterized by aggressive pawn play.
  • Closed Sicilian: Used by positional players.
